Ampere's Circuital Law

 
 


 
 

The ampere’s circuital law is the magnetostatics version of the Gauss’s law for Electrostatics. Ampere’s circuital law states that “the line integral of the Magnetic field intensity H about any closed path is equal to the direct current enclosed by that path”.
