Inductors in Series & Parallel

 
 


 
 

  • Inductors in Series

For inductors in series, the total inductance is simply the sum of individual inductances, just as with resistors in series.





  • Inductors in Parallel

For a parallel combination of inductors, the reciprocal of the equivalent inductance is the sum of the reciprocals of the individual inductances, just as with resistors in parallel.





Do note that these results are under the assumption that the magnetic fields of the inductors do not interact with each other.
