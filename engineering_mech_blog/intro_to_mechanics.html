Introduction to Mechanics

 
 


 
 

Mechanics is a branch of physics that deals bodies at rest or in motion under the influence of forces. The word "Mechanics" is derived from the greek word "mechane", which means machine. It is one of the oldest, if not the oldest branches of physical science. And without a doubt the most important one. The main objective of mechanics is to analyse and predict the physical behaviour of objects and thereby make them suitable for engineering applications.

The applications of Mechanics are vast and it forms the basis for several other fields of studies such as,


  • Structural engineering

  • Aerodynamics

  • Electrical Machines

  • Machine design

  • Robotics

  • Rocketry etc.

Basically all the cool stuff ;)


Based on the nature of the bodies involved, this field of study can be classified into Solid & Fluid Mechanics. In this particular blog, we are interested in Solid Mechanics with the emphasis on its Engineering applications.
